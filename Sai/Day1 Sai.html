Mathematics Question Paper

Formative Assessment-2023

Class : 10

Max.Marks : 20

* Answer Any four questions of the following

1.Find the sum of first 24 terms of an A.P. whose nth term is given by a2=3+2n
2.If the coordinates of two points are A(3,4), B (5,-2) and a point P (x,5) is such that PA = PB, then find the area of P delta B
3.A solid metallic cylinder of diameter 12 cm and height 15 cm is melted and recast into toys in the shape of a cone of radius 3 cm and height 9 cm. Find the number of toys so formed.
4.From a pack of 52 playing cards, Jacks and Kings of red colour and Queens and Aces of black colour are removed. The remaining cards are mixed and a card is drawn at random. Find the probability that the drawn card is
(i) a black Queen
(ii) a card of red colour
(iii) a Jack of black colour
(iv) a face card
5.Prove that the lengths of tangents drawn from an external point to a circle are equal.
6.Prove that the parallelogram circumscribing a circle is a rhombus.
